RECALIBERATE THE COMPASS BY FILLING UP ALL LEDS, THEN PRESS A
PRESS A AND B TOGETHER TO RECALIBERATE AGAIN
PRESS A TO SEE HOW FAR FROM NORTH YOU ARE
PRESS B TO PLACE A TREASURE
ROTATE THE MICROBIT TO FIND THE TREASURE
A TICK MEANS YOU FOUND IT
A CROSS MEANS, IT IS IN A DIFFERENT PLACE
PRESS B AGAIN TO SET THE TREASURE AT A DIFFERENT LOCATION
